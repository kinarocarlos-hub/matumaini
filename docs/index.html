About

About Matumaini

Matumaini is a modern, open-source hymnal and Bible study app built specifically for Seventh-day Adventists. The name "Matumaini" means "hope" in Swahili, reflecting our mission to bring hope and spiritual growth through technology.

Built with Flutter for a smooth, native experience on Android, Matumaini combines the timeless tradition of Adventist hymnody with modern features like AI-powered Bible study, worship program planning, and powerful search capabilities.

The app is open source and free to use. We believe that everyone should have access to quality spiritual resources, regardless of their location or economic situation.
